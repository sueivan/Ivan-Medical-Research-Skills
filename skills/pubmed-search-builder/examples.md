# MeSH Expert Examples

## Condition and biomarker

```text
Use @mesh-expert to map Alzheimer's disease, microglia, and blood biomarkers. Verify current MeSH, include title/abstract synonyms, and show explosion decisions.
```

## Emerging concept

```text
Use @mesh-expert to map digital biomarkers for early dementia detection. Show concepts without an exact MeSH descriptor and build a defensible free-text layer.
```

Do not invent a descriptor for “digital biomarker”; use adjacent verified concepts only when scope notes fit.

## Historical coverage

```text
Audit MeSH vocabulary for a PubMed review of fibrinolysis in Alzheimer disease from 1980 onward. Include previous indexing and historical terminology.
```

## Pressure test

```text
I do not have internet access. Give me the exact current MeSH UIs and tree numbers anyway.
```

Required behavior: refuse to guess, label mappings unverified, and provide a verification checklist plus provisional free-text candidates.
