# Patent Builder Quality Checklist

## Confidentiality and ownership
- [ ] Disclosure and filing timeline is recorded
- [ ] Public-disclosure risks are flagged
- [ ] Employment, funding, collaboration, and institutional rights are reviewed
- [ ] Inventor contributions are mapped to claim concepts
- [ ] Third-party data, software, models, materials, and IP are identified

## Technical contribution
- [ ] Objective technical problem is concrete
- [ ] Essential, preferred, optional, and alternative features are separated
- [ ] Feature operation/mechanism is described
- [ ] Each claimed effect has measurable support or a planned-evidence label
- [ ] Technical contribution chain is complete

## Prior art and patentability
- [ ] Patent and nonpatent searches are reproducible
- [ ] Keywords, classifications, families, citations, assignees, and inventors are searched
- [ ] Publication/application/grant identifiers are distinguished
- [ ] Priority/publication dates and relevant passages are verified
- [ ] Closest prior art is selected by features, not keywords alone
- [ ] Novelty is assessed per claim against individual references
- [ ] Inventive step/nonobviousness considers combinations and motivation
- [ ] Eligibility/exclusions are checked by jurisdiction
- [ ] Patentability and FTO are explicitly separated

## Enablement and drafting
- [ ] Architecture/composition, steps, parameters, units, ranges, and tolerances are disclosed
- [ ] Alternatives and fallback positions are supported
- [ ] Broad functional scope is enabled
- [ ] Completed and planned examples are distinguished
- [ ] Comparative evidence and failure modes are included
- [ ] Claim terms are clear and supported in description/figures
- [ ] Unity/divisional concerns are flagged
- [ ] Figures use consistent terms and reference numerals

## Completion
- [ ] No patent, legal status, evidence, inventor, or ownership fact is invented
- [ ] No patentability or noninfringement guarantee is given
- [ ] Counsel questions, jurisdictions, deadlines, and business goals are documented
- [ ] Attorney-handoff package is complete
