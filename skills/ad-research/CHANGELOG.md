# Changelog

## 0.1.0 — 2026-07-12

- Replaced the scaffold with an operational Alzheimer disease domain orchestrator.
- Added disease-continuum, phenotype/pathology/biomarker, domain, evidence-level, heterogeneity, and translation-maturity frameworks.
- Added workflows for mechanisms, model systems, biomarkers, risk/prevention, drugs, nonpharmacological interventions, complementary medicine, care, bibliometrics, and public communication.
- Added safeguards against single-cause claims, animal-to-human leaps, biomarker utility overstatement, network-pharmacology overclaiming, and citation-based clinical inference.
- Added AD research brief, translation matrix, and claim-boundary log.
