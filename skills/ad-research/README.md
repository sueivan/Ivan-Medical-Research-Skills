# Alzheimer Disease Research

Status: **operational domain orchestrator (v0.1.0)**

`ad-research` structures Alzheimer disease and related dementia research by disease stage, phenotype/pathology/biomarker definition, evidence level, translational maturity, and intended use.

## Main functions

- refine AD research questions
- route searches, reviews, bibliometrics, verification, extraction, review, and public communication
- assess mechanisms, models, biomarkers, risk, prevention, treatments, care, and implementation
- prevent preclinical, biomarker, bibliometric, and statistical findings from being overstated clinically

## Invoke

```text
Use @ad-research to plan [research product] on [AD topic]. Define the disease stage, diagnostic and biomarker basis, evidence type, outcomes, translational maturity, IMRS route, and claims that the evidence can and cannot support.
```

Current guideline, approval, label, trial, and clinical-availability information must be verified live.
