# Grant Builder Quality Checklist

## Call and fit
- [ ] Official current call and access date are recorded
- [ ] Applicant/institution eligibility is verified
- [ ] Funding, duration, costs, limits, forms, deadline, and scoring are extracted
- [ ] Go/no-go assessment is documented
- [ ] Funder priorities and project product align

## Evidence and aims
- [ ] Problem magnitude is verified and relevant
- [ ] Evidence gap is decision specific
- [ ] Closest prior work is compared
- [ ] Innovation is classified and not label based
- [ ] Central question and 2–4 aims are coherent
- [ ] Aims are not merely project phases
- [ ] Aim dependencies and contingencies are credible

## Methods and rigor
- [ ] Design matches every aim
- [ ] Population, setting, sampling, recruitment, and retention are feasible
- [ ] Sample-size assumptions and calculations are sourced
- [ ] Primary/secondary outcomes and time points are explicit
- [ ] Instruments and procedures are valid and reproducible
- [ ] Analysis addresses design, missingness, clustering, multiplicity, and sensitivity
- [ ] Rigor, validation, quality assurance, and reproducibility are addressed
- [ ] Specialist review is obtained where needed

## Feasibility and governance
- [ ] Preliminary data are authentic and qualified
- [ ] Facilities, participant/data access, and partnerships are verified or marked pending
- [ ] Team expertise maps to aims and risks
- [ ] Ethics, consent, privacy, safety, and data governance are credible
- [ ] Work packages include owners, milestones, deliverables, and acceptance criteria
- [ ] Risks have prevention, contingency, owner, and residual risk

## Budget, impact, and compliance
- [ ] Every cost maps to a task and allowable rule
- [ ] Rates, quantities, arithmetic, caps, overhead, and cost sharing are checked
- [ ] Timeline reflects dependencies and approvals
- [ ] Outputs, outcomes, and long-term impacts are separated
- [ ] Beneficiaries and adoption pathways are explicit
- [ ] Dissemination exceeds publication alone
- [ ] Title, summary, aims, methods, timeline, budget, and impact agree
- [ ] Page/character limits, attachments, signatures, and system fields comply
