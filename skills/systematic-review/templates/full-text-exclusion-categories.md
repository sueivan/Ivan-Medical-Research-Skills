# Full-Text Exclusion Categories

Customize and operationalize before screening. Assign one primary reason per excluded report.

1. Ineligible population
2. Ineligible intervention/exposure
3. Ineligible comparator
4. Ineligible outcome
5. Ineligible study design
6. Ineligible setting/context
7. Outside date/language/publication-status criteria
8. Not an original eligible report
9. Duplicate report
10. Insufficient information after retrieval/contact attempts
11. Full text unavailable after documented attempts

Order categories according to a prespecified hierarchy to improve consistency. Do not hide multiple reports from the same eligible study as “duplicates.”
