# VOSviewer Expert Quality Checklist

## Corpus
- [ ] Database, collection/indexes, query, timespan, and search date are recorded
- [ ] Raw files are preserved
- [ ] Export batches are complete and nonoverlapping
- [ ] Required cited references/affiliations/abstracts/keywords are present
- [ ] Record totals and encoding are checked
- [ ] Duplicate and early-access policies are stated

## Analysis design
- [ ] Research question matches analysis type
- [ ] Unit of analysis is precise
- [ ] Link and total link strength meanings are stated
- [ ] VOSviewer version is recorded
- [ ] Full/fractional counting is justified
- [ ] Threshold and selection rule are recorded
- [ ] Normalization method is recorded
- [ ] Clustering settings are recorded
- [ ] Term source and relevance selection are recorded

## Cleaning
- [ ] Thesaurus version is preserved
- [ ] Raw-to-replacement mappings are auditable
- [ ] Related but distinct concepts are not merged for convenience
- [ ] Author homonyms/name variants are assessed
- [ ] Institution hierarchy and aliases are assessed
- [ ] Country/region rules are explicit
- [ ] Search-imposed/generic terms are handled transparently

## Interpretation
- [ ] Items, links, and total link strengths are reported correctly
- [ ] Map distance is not treated as raw strength
- [ ] Clusters are validated against content
- [ ] Overlay variable and scale are explicit
- [ ] Average year is not called exact emergence year
- [ ] Density weight is explicit
- [ ] Citation lag and partial years are acknowledged
- [ ] Bibliometric prominence is not called evidence quality

## Sensitivity and completion
- [ ] Consequential alternative settings are compared
- [ ] Major conclusions are checked for stability
- [ ] Map, network, and item outputs are preserved
- [ ] Figures explain encodings and parameters
- [ ] No output or metric is invented
- [ ] Another researcher could reconstruct the analysis
