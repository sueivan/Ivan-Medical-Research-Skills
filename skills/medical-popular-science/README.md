# Medical Popular Science

Status: **operational beta (v0.1.0)**

`medical-popular-science` converts verified biomedical evidence into accurate, readable public communication while preserving uncertainty, evidence level, risk, and clinical boundaries.

## Main outputs

- audience and communication brief
- claim–evidence ledger
- evidence-stage and clinical-distance assessment
- outline with AI-template risk points
- article, book section, social copy, or script
- risk/misinterpretation audit
- verified references
- visual brief and caption

## Invoke

```text
Use @medical-popular-science to explain [topic] to [audience]. Verify every major claim, distinguish association from causation and preclinical from clinical evidence, analyze article logic before drafting, and provide verified references.
```

This Skill explains evidence and must not be used as individualized medical advice.
