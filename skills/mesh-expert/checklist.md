# MeSH Expert Quality Checklist

## Scope
- [ ] Research question and database are stated
- [ ] Essential concept blocks are defined
- [ ] Ambiguous meanings are separated
- [ ] Sensitivity/precision priority is recorded

## Verification
- [ ] Every descriptor is checked against an authoritative NLM record
- [ ] Preferred descriptor, UI, scope note, and verification date are recorded
- [ ] Entry terms are not mislabeled as descriptors
- [ ] Tree positions and narrower terms are inspected
- [ ] Introduction date and previous indexing are considered when relevant
- [ ] Qualifiers are verified when used

## Coverage
- [ ] Controlled vocabulary and free text are both present
- [ ] Emerging and unindexed concepts are covered
- [ ] Indexing lag is considered
- [ ] Explosion decisions are explicit
- [ ] Rejected terms have reasons

## Integrity and completion
- [ ] Nothing is invented
- [ ] Unverified mappings are labeled
- [ ] Seed-article checks are documented
- [ ] Unresolved issues are reported
- [ ] Another researcher could reconstruct the mapping
- [ ] Output is ready for `pubmed-search-builder`
