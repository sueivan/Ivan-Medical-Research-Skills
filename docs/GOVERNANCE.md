# Governance

## Maintainer

Project maintainer: Ivan Su (`@sueivan`)

## Decision principles

1. scientific integrity over convenience;
2. reproducibility over visual polish;
3. narrow reliable skills over broad vague skills;
4. source verification over confident wording;
5. documented correction over silent revision.

## Acceptance

Contributions may be rejected when they are redundant, unverifiable, overbroad, or likely to produce misleading medical research outputs.
